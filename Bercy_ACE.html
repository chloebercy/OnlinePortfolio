Name : Entreprenuership Project 2018

Course : Principles of Engineering

Date : May 21, 2018

In this project, my partner's and my goal was to create a solution that relieved academic or school related stress. Our end product was a website that could calculate the user's GPA, how much their grade will drop/increase, the required score needed on a test to change their grade to a certain percentage and the required score needed on the final to change their grade to a certain percentage. Other tasks our website can do includes finding college courses based off your interest and finding colleges based of your SAT scores, ACT scores or GPA. During this project, my main responsibility was the mathematical aspect of the solution. I found the formulas, created all the pages dealing calculations, and created the homepage/instructions. I also contributed to the documentation (which can be found here). Our project was graded using this rubric. Feel free to check out our project here.
