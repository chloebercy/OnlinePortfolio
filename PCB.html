CHLOE BERCY


Name : Printed Circuit Board Project

Course : Honors Digital Electronics

Date : October 26, 2019

In this project, I created a printed circuit board. This process included creating the design of the board, printing + masking the design, etching, drilling and soldering the components. The circuit was designed to be russian roulette game, somewhat like the toy, Crocodile Dentist. It consists of a 555 timer, a decade counter, OR gates, switches and LEDS. The biggest problem faced was while creating the design for the board. While transferring my circuit from the simulated design on Multisim and a cirucit on a breadboard, I made an error while creating and simplifying the design on a printed circuit board.
